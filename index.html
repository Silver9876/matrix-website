ABOUT US

Avinya, a national-level technical festival, is a testament to the zeal of the CSE students at JNTUH College of Engineering Manthani. Every year, the department transforms into a vibrant hub, welcoming students from across India. Avinya transcends the boundaries of textbooks, hosting a diverse array of technical and non-technical events. Whether you're a coding whiz or a creative mind, Avinya offers a platform to showcase your talents. From thrilling hackathons and algorithm challenges to captivating workshops and design competitions, Avinya promises an experience that ignites passion and fosters knowledge sharing.
